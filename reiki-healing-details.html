What is Reiki?

Reiki is a Japanese healing technique that means "universal life energy." Reiki works with the natural energy that flows through all living beings to promote healing, balance, and well-being.

During a Reiki session, practitioners channel this universal life force energy through their hands to activate your body's natural healing processes. This gentle, non-invasive practice helps remove energetic blockages, reduce stress, and restore balance to your physical, emotional, and spiritual being.

Reiki works on the principle that healing energy flows where it is needed most, addressing the root cause of imbalance rather than just symptoms. This intelligent energy knows exactly what you need and provides it in the perfect way for your highest good.

How Reiki Works

1. Energy Channeling

The practitioner acts as a conduit for universal life force energy, allowing it to flow through their hands to you.

2. Energetic Balancing

Reiki energy flows to areas of imbalance, clearing blockages and restoring natural energy flow throughout your system.

3. Natural Healing

Your body's innate healing abilities are activated, promoting recovery, relaxation, and overall well-being.

What to Expect

Before Your Session

Come in comfortable clothing and with an open heart. We'll discuss your intentions and any areas where you'd like healing focus. No special preparation is needed - just bring yourself as you are.

During Your Session

You'll lie fully clothed on a comfortable massage table while I gently place my hands on or slightly above specific points on your body. You may feel warmth, tingling, deep relaxation, or emotional release. Some people see colors or have spiritual insights.

After Your Session

Many people feel deeply relaxed, energized, or both. The healing continues after your session, so stay hydrated and be gentle with yourself. Some may experience emotional releases or shifts in the days following treatment.

Reiki is Safe for Everyone

Reiki is completely safe and non-invasive. It can be received by people of all ages and conditions, including pregnant women, children, and those with serious illnesses. Reiki complements but never replaces medical treatment.
