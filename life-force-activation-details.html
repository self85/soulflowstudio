What is Source Transmission - Life Force Activation?

This is an intuitive, experience-based practice designed to awaken something deep within you - helping you remember who you truly are beyond conditioned patterns and limitations. The gentle yet powerful activation works by gradually building energy in a way that honors your system's capacity, ensuring you receive exactly what you need without overwhelming your nervous system.

What to Expect

Before Your Session

We'll begin with a short introduction. Please wear comfortable clothing and come with an open mind and heart.

Preparation for Online Sessions

  • Download the Zoom app. You'll receive a Zoom link via email approximately 15 minutes before we start.
  • Prepare a comfortable space where you can lie down undisturbed. Position your computer/iPad or phone so we can see your entire body (either from the side or from your head).
  • We recommend using external speakers or headphones for the best audio quality. Test the technology beforehand to ensure everything works.
  • Wear comfortable, loose clothing.
  • Eat lightly before the session - the experience is usually strongest on an empty stomach.
  • Avoid caffeine during the day.

During Your Session

You'll lie comfortably while I guide the energy transmission. Nothing is required from you, simply to relax and allow. The activation may create spontaneous movements, emotional releases, or deep relaxation states. Each session is different, and every participant's experience is unique. Trust whatever arises - your body knows exactly what it needs.

After Your Session

Integration is a crucial part of the healing process. You may continue to feel shifts and releases for days or weeks following your session. Stay hydrated, rest when needed, and be gentle with yourself as your system recalibrates.

Important Considerations

Not suitable if you are:

  • Pregnant in the 3rd trimester
  • Under 18 years old
  • Under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Have epilepsy or a history of psychosis
